Profile summary

My interests and career revolve around the topic of web technologies. I started as an editor, copywriter and translator for a variety of web portals and agencies. Soon I learned more about online marketing and advertising as well as about SEO and webmastering. I'm deeply interested and invloved into the topic of tech start-ups. For the past two years I've been publishing intensively in Polish and English for new tech portals news.bitspiration.com and the governmental web.gov.pl (English version on web.gov.pl/en). Aside of journalism, I love writing efficient and SEO optimized copy.

I'm also learning web development intensively. I combine front-end development prowess with the knowledge and experience of SEO copywriter who loves enriching self-coded project with copy that sells and inspires.

Those three skills - tech journalism, web copywriting and web development - are those I want to work on today, tommorow and for good.
